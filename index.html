Comparative Study of Machine Learning Algorithms on Sentiment Analysis of Product Reviews Spring 2019 Making machines understand human sentiments would allow us to analyse millions of product reviews that exist on sites like Amazon. The text reviews were preprocessed and the Bag of Words model was used for feature extraction. We applied various Machine learning algorithms such as Naive Bayes, SVM, Random Forest, etc. and analysed the results. We created a voted classifier using the mode of the votes of the ML models to obtain a better-performing classifier.
