About Me

I'm Amitabha, a Master's student in Robotics at the University of Minnesota. My background blends electronics, computer science, and robotics. I love diving deep into programming languages like Python, C, Java, and R, and I'm pretty handy with data science tools like PyTorch, TensorFlow, and scikit-learn.

I've also dabbled in robotic applications, such as using Baxter to sort boxes autonomously and planning paths for mobile robots.

With a blend of technical skills and hands-on experience, I'm all set to take on challenging problems in robotics, computer vision, and beyond. I'm particularly interested in pushing the boundaries of what's possible with intelligent systems and creating innovative solutions that make a difference.
